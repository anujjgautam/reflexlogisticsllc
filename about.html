Company Overview

Reflex Logistics is a premier provider of supply chain solutions to automotive, consumer and industrial markets. Our unique combination of order management, distribution & fulfillment, customs brokerage and freight transportation creates a comprehensive solution that addresses your global logistics needs.

Our team of experts has decades of experience working with some of the largest companies in the industry, allowing us to provide you with a highly customized solution tailored to your company's unique needs.

We are committed to excellence and integrity by delivering results that are not only on-time but also with exceptional quality at all times.

We will take care of your cargo or your passenger and deliver them safe and on time.

At Reflex Logistics, ensuring the safety of our customers, employees and our communities is our priority. We understand the importance of continuous training and are proud of our safety knowledge, experienced staff and ability to exceed industry standards year after year. We have established and continually maintain excellent motor carrier safety ratings and low accident frequencies.

As a company, we have a solid safety performance history and will continue to be a leader in the area of safety and compliance, due to the dedication and professionalism of our fleet of drivers and vehicle maintenance personnel.

At Reflex Logistics, we offer industry-leading asset protection and security compliance programs.

Reflex Logistics understands that our customers may have important and unique needs related to homeland security regulatory compliance, high-risk products, or brand protection. We offer consultation and proactive partnership to ensure that our customers’ security needs are met.

By leveraging modern and proven technologies, we provide for the integrity of customer assets while in-transit or at one of our facilities.
